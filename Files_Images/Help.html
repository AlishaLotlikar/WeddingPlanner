How to Use Our website:



Last updated: 19/11/17

We provide 'THE BEST' vendors from all over Goa for you to plan a successful wedding.
You have to first sign up to avail any services provided by us. After logging in you can view all the vendors and packages provided by our Wedding Planners. You can then select the desired vendor and get the available slot for you. Click onto the 'Add Cart button to add your desired packages or vendors' You can view your cart using the cart tab. You can choose your desired mode of payment and proceed with the payments. A bill will be generated depending on your total cost. Hope you are satisfied with our help.
